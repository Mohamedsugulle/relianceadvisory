About Us

Reliance Advisory Services LLP is a dynamic professional services firm headquartered in Garowe, Somalia, with strategic branches in Nairobi, Mogadishu, and Garowe. We specialize in providing tailored solutions in finance, legal advisory, audit, technology, and capacity development. With a strong commitment to excellence, we empower public and private institutions to achieve financial accountability, drive digital transformation, and foster institutional growth. Our multidisciplinary team brings together local insight and global standards, ensuring that each client receives innovative, high-quality, and impactful support. At Reliance Advisory, we believe in building long-term partnerships rooted in trust, integrity, and measurable results.

Our Vision

To be the leading advisory firm in the Horn of Africa, transforming institutions through innovation, integrity, and local excellence aligned with global standards.

Our Mission

To empower governments, businesses, and civil society organizations by delivering trusted solutions in finance, legal advisory, technology, and institutional development—creating sustainable impact for generations to come.
